World Clock

For this project, I immersed myself in the intricate tapestry of updating the date and time with Javascript, by developing a World Clock. Harnessing the power of moment.js, this project showcases the ability to display times according to their location and global time zone.
Central to the experience is a user-friendly select element that empowers you to check the current time by effortlessly choosing from a variety of locations all over the world.
